About This Calculator

Prototype for the web-based risk stratification model in "Risk Stratification Model for Predicting Late-Onset Preeclampsia Using Clinical and Laboratory Data from Filipino Pregnant Women Admitted to WVSUMC" (MG 4, WVSUMC-COM, February 2026).

Uses retrospective cohort design with multivariable logistic regression and bootstrap internal validation (TRIPOD, 1,000 replications). Current weights are proxy thresholds from literature. Final β coefficients replace these post-study.
